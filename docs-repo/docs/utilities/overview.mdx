NUO-STEMS 4 includes **Utilities**: tools that work alongside the main conversion modes. They are grouped in the app under the **Utilities** area (with Traktor Pro 4– and Serato-related actions).

### Traktor Pro 4

- [**Migrate .stem.m4a (created in NUO-STEMS 4) to natively linked TP4 stems**](/docs/utilities/migrate-stem-m4a-to-tp4) — use with [Traktor Pro 4 Legacy / Export Mode](/docs/getting-started/legacy-traktor) to migrate `.stem.m4a` files from another computer (for example a gaming PC) to linked stems in your Traktor collection on your DJ laptop.
- [**Extract individual stems from TP4 .stem.m4a**](/docs/utilities/extract-individual-stems-from-stem-m4a) — export WAV stems from TP4 `.stem.m4a` / `.stem.mp4` containers.
- [**Change TP4 STEMS colors for previously converted TP4 native stems**](/docs/utilities/change-stems-colors) — update drums, bass, other, and vocals colors on TP4 native stems that were already converted.

### Serato DJ

- [**Extract individual stems from .serato-stems**](/docs/utilities/extract-individual-stems-from-serato-stems) — export stems as **`.mp3`** files from `.serato-stems` created with NUO-STEMS 4 Serato integration.

:::info
Utilities in NUO-STEMS 4 are **free to use** and do not require a license.
:::
