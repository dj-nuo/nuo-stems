### Available Utilities

- [**Migrate .stem.m4a (created in NUO-STEMS 4) to natively linked TP4 stems**](/docs/traktor-utilities/migrate-stem-m4a-to-tp4) - used with [Legacy / Export Mode](/docs/getting-started/legacy-traktor) to migrate .stem.m4a files generated on your Gaming PC to linked stems on your DJ Laptop Traktor collection.
- [**Extract individual stems from .stem.m4a**](/docs/traktor-utilities/extract-individual-stems-from-stem-m4a) - Extract individual WAV stem files from .stem.m4a/.stem.mp4 containers
- [**Change STEMS colors for previously converted files**](/docs/traktor-utilities/change-stems-colors) - Update colors of individual STEMS parts (drums, bass, other, vocals) for files that were already converted

:::info
**Traktor Utilities** features in NUO-STEMS 4 are **free to use** and do not require a license.
:::
