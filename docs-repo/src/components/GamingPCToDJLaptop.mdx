
### I want to generate stems on my Gaming PC and then use them on my DJ Laptop. Can I do this?
Yes, you can use [Traktor Pro 4 Legacy / Export Mode](/docs/getting-started/legacy-traktor) to generate .stem.m4a files on your Gaming PC.
Then you can use [Utilities to migrate `.stem.m4a`](/docs/utilities/migrate-stem-m4a-to-tp4) files to linked stems on your DJ Laptop Traktor collection.
Using Utilities does not require a paid license.
